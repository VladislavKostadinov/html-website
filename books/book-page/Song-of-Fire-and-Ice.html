Song of Fire and Ice

Song of Fire and Ice by J. R. R. Martin is a bestseller which got its very own tv series spin-off, which grew in popularity. Many of the fans know about the story from the tv series, still I loved it even before HBO aired it, and was thrilled that it got its own visual adaptation. The books are awesome and combine realism with fantasy in a unique way I find interesting. Charecters and events are unpredictable, heroes rise and fall, dragons soar through the skies. I grew affinity to it in a way, I would rather keep to myself, but I do appreciate the author's great contribution to the fantasy genre.
