Mid period

A couple of years have passed and I had new "roomates". They tend to make me feel calm and happy, when I watch them swin arround in harmony, or jump playfully into the air, before submergin into the water again. My friends are friendly to one another and accept new inhabitants with hospitality. There are some shrimps as well, and also some fresh-water snails. The vegetation is growing fine, some plants dissapear only to immerge once again from the dirt in unexpected place. I suspect that underground they have developed a network of roots.
